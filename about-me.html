I am a qualified winemaker and graphic designer currently living in Washington State, USA. When I’m not at the winery or behind a computer, you’ll find me adventuring by foot or kayak through the mountains of PNW.

My Background

A Love of Wine

Originally from Wisconsin, I pursued my interests in science and technology studying Biochemistry and Computer Science at Colby College in Waterville, ME. During my time at Colby I fell in love with New Zealand while studying abroad and decided to return after graduating. Having grown up in a family of restaurateurs, I had learned to revere wine even before I could drink it, so upon moving to Marlborough, NZ, I pursued a career in their wine industry. Applying my BA in Biochemistry to this field, I earned a Graduate Diploma in Viticulture and Oenology from Lincoln University near Christchurch, NZ, in 2014. I have since worked primarily with Sauvignon Blanc, Pinot Noir, Riesling, and Chardonnay from the Awatere Valley subregion of Marlborough.

In 2018, I returned to the United States and relocated to Washington State where I dove into the American wine industry and Washington reds. I joined Upchurch Vineyard as Assistant Winemaker in August 2019 and currently hold down the fort at their Eastern Washington winery on Red Mountain.

"I have always loved that winemaking combines art, science, and sociability. The wines of Red Mountain showcase this confluence perfectly."

A Passion for Design

The fortunate daughter of a creative mother, I grew up helping with interior decorating projects, holiday staging, and retail displays. In 2017, out of personal interest in another form of design, I completed a Certificate IV in Graphic Design through The Graphic Design School in Avalon, New South Wales, Australia. However, having awoken a passion for design and realizing its applicability to my work as a winemaker at a small family business, I am currently pursuing a Certificate in Front-End Development offered online through the University of Washington.
